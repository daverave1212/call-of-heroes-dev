#Background#
Acolyte
Servant of the Temple

#BackgroundDescription#
A man of the church.
A mistress of the gods.
Or a corrupt priest overcome by gluttony and greed.
You have spent your life in the service of a temple to a specific god or pantheon of gods. Choose a god, a pantheon of gods, or some other quasi-divine being, and work with your DM to detail the nature of your religious service.
You must choose a god to follow.

#StartingItems#
	Holy Symbol
	5 Sticks of Incense
	Book of Prayers
	A bag containing 3d10 (15) gold

#Skills#
You have +1 Investigation, +1 Religion and +1 Performance
You have -1 Intimidation, -1 Biology and -1 Luck

#Abilities#
[Acolyte] : Passive
You must choose a god to follow, from the Call of Heroes pantheon or your DM's world's pantheon.
You have 50% discount on items specifically related to religion.
You can get free (minimum necessary) food, water and shelter at churches or temples specific to your god for all of your party.

[Divine Sense] : 1 Action
Spend 1 Charge.
You know the locations and types of all Holy, Unholy and Celestial creatures or things within 15 meters, unless the path to them is obstructed by thick or dense walls or magic.
You can do this once per rest without using a Charge.

#Background#
Charlatan
"People's Person"

#BackgroundDescription#
You know what people want and you deliver, or rather, you promise to deliver.
You have always had a way with people. You know what makes them tick. It's a useful talent, and one that you're perfectly willing to use for your advantage.

#StartingItems#
	Cheating tools of your choice, such as wieghted dice, unusual coins or deck of cards, etc
	Fine Clothes
	A bag containing 9d10 (45) gold

#Skills#
You have +1 Sleight of Hand, +1 Deception and +1 Performance
You have -1 Luck, -1 Animal Handling and -1 Intimidation

#Abilities#
[Charlatan] : Passive
You have +2 on Sleight of Hand checks when trying to cheat at games.
Once per week, if in a developed city, you can roll a Sleight of Hand check and gain gold equal to what you rolled.

[Disguise] : 1 hour
Using 50 gold worth of materials, you can disguise yourself to look differently. If you have to roll for this, roll Deception with a +3 bonus.


#Background#
Criminal
Law Breaker

#BackgroundDescription#
You're far closer than most people to the world of murder, theft, and violence that pervades the underbelly of civilization, and you have survived up to this point by flouting the rules and regulations of society.

#StartingItems#

	Dark Hood
	Pocket Knife
	Crowbar
	25 Fake Gold Coins
	A bag containing 8d10 gold

#Skills#
You have +1 Stealth, +1 Deception and +1 Investigation
You have -1 History, -1 Performance and -1 Religion

#Abilities#
[Criminal] : Passive
You can gather information on criminal activities by spending one day in a city, investigating it.
You have +2 on Persuasion when trying to negotiate an illegal deal.

[Stalk] : Depends
Pick a person you can see. If the target is alone, you can follow it and know it's location for up to 4 hours, without being detected (in normal conditions).

#Background#
Entertainer
The Center of Attention

#BackgroundDescription#
We don't make mistakes, just happy little accidents.
You thrive in front of an audience with you majestic lute, stunts or vocal mastery. Whatever techniques you use, your art is your life.

#StartingItems#

	Instrument of Choice
	Entertainer Costume
	Pocket Knife
	A bag containing 4d10 gold

#Skills#
You have +1 Luck, +1 Acrobatics and +1 Performance
You have -1 Survival, -1 Animal Handling and -1 Intimidation

#Abilities#
[Entertain] : 4 hours
You must have an entertaining method of chioce.
Once per day, if you are in a tavern, you can roll a Performance check. You gain (Rolled / 4) gold for entertaning the public.

[Sense Mood] : 1 Action
Pick a target you can see and hear. You can tell the target's mood, such as if it's angry, sad, happy, in love, etc.

[Rest Performance] : Passive
After every long rest with you, all allies have +1 maximum health until their next long rest.


#Background#
Folk Hero
Champion of the People

#BackgroundDescription#
Destiny calls you to stand against the tyrants and monsters that threaten the common folk!
You previously pursued a simple profession among the peasantry, perhaps as a farmer, miner, servant, shepherd, woodcutter, or gravedigger. But something happened that set you on a different path and marked you for greater things.

#StartingItems#

	A Riding Horse or a non-combat dog
	Clothes, soap and deodorant
	Hatchet
	A bag containing 4d10 gold

#Skills#
You have +1 Animal Handling, +1 Survival and +1 Luck
You have -1 Deception, -1 Arcana and -1 Stealth

#Abilities#
[Folk Hero] : Passive
Once per week, you can request prosivions from local folk, such as food, water and shelter for up to 3 days, new clothes.
You know the locations of taverns, inns and other such places around.
You get 50% discount on all services provided at taverns.
You have -5 on Deception rolls agains common folk.

The Living Myth

#BackgroundDescription#
Legend says about you and your malevolent deeds, about your plots and mischieves...
You are notorious among common folk. People have heard about you, but many have not seen it with their own eyes. They talk about the lord in the black temple, the lone savage in the forest or the wizard atop the tower, in the center of the town.

#StartingItems#

	A bird or cat, non-combat companion (non-magical)
	A bag containing 2d20 gold

#Skills#
You have +1 Knowledge, +1 Stealth and +1 Investigation
You have -1 Luck, -1 Persuasion and -1 Sleight of Hand

#Abilities#
[Folk Villain] : Passive
You have +2 Stealth against common folk and +2 on Knowledge regarding local myths, legends and problems.

[Special Mark] : 1 Action
You can leave a special mark on a tree, rock or ground. Those who see that mark are less likely to continue going ahead.

#Background#
Knight
Paragon of Chivalry

#BackgroundDescription#
A privilege to be respected by others, but also to respect yourself.
A knight is a person granted an honorary title of knighthood by a monarch, bishop or noble, in return of your total servitude, in both combat and noble duty.

#StartingItems#

	Riding Horse
	Banner
	Symbol or Signet
	Special Decorative Shield
	A bag containing 10d10 gold

#Skills#
You have +1 Athletics, +1 History and +1 Performance
You have -1 Deception, -1 Nature and -1 Craft

#Abilities#
[Knight] : Passive
You serve or used to serve as the Knight of a noble or important person.
You have 25% discount on buying horses or mounts.
You also have 25% discount on blacksmith services.

[Vow] : A Lifetime
You may vow upon the completion of a non-trivial mission or task. Succeeding rewards you with 1 Inspiration. Failing takes away 1 Inspiration, and your Inspiration can go below 0. If you have 0 or negative Inspiration, you have to gain Inspiration to compensate until you reach 1 to be able to use it again.

Outlander
The Lone Wolf

#BackgroundDescription#
Few live to see the beauty of these lands, for they can't see the forest from the trees.
You grew up in the wilds. You've witnessed the migration of herds larger than forests, survived weather more extreme than any city-dweller could comprehend, and enjoyed the solitude of being the only thinking creature for miles in any direction.

#StartingItems#

	Rags
	Makeshift Hunting Trap
	Quarterstaff

#Skills#
You have +1 Athletics, +1 Survival, +1 Nature and +1 Biology
You have -1 Knowledge, -1 History, -1 Deception and -1 Persuasion

#Abilities#
[Outlander] : Passive
Choose a terrain type (ex: forest, desert, mountains). While in that terrain type, you can only get lost by magical means.
When in an unknown territory, you can roll a Perception check. If you succeed, you know whether you are trespassing or not.

[Procure Item] : 4 Hours
Spend 1 Charge.
Roll a Craft check.
You can create (rolled check / 5) items from the surroundings (if the environment is proper).
These can be mundane items, such as torches, rations, even rope.
