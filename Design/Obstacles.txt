[Chandelier]
On damage, it drops, exploding for 3d6 damage (vs defense) in a 3x3 area.

[Pit] (3/6/9 meters)

[Pressure Plate]

[Net Trap] [Web Trap]
Creature must succeed DC12 Dex save or be Snared.

[Fog/Magical Darkness]
Attacks from inside the darkness have Cover.
Certain enemies are not affected (vampires, liches, etc)

[Cursed Obelisk]
A creature entering its adjacenty takes 1d6 Necrotic damage.
Have 5 HP, 5 Armor.

[Explosion Rune]
When touched, the toucher takes 2d6 damage.

[Caltrops]
Unit takes 1 damage for each square it touches.

[Fire] [Toxic Gas] [Electric Water]
Unit takes 2 damage for each square it touches.

[Lava]
Unit takes 4 damage for each lava square it touches.

[Spiked Barricade]
Unit pushed into it takes 2d6 damage (vs Defense).
Failing an Acrobatics to jump over deals 1d6 damage.

[Oil Lamp] [Poison Vase]
Destroyer rolls a DC12 Luck check.
If it succeeds, the lamp explodes for 3d6 Fire damage (vs Defense) in a 2x2 area and leaves the area with Fire for 1 minute.
Else, it just leaves a 2x2 oil area.
The oil area, ignited or not, is Hard Terrain.

[Spider Egg]
