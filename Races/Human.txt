#Race#
	Human
Humans are probably the most versatile of races, and that comes with its own advantages and disadvantages.

#Options#
When creating your character:
	a) Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Fortitude, Dexterity, Wisdom, Charisma and Intelligence
	b) You start with 2 extra Feat Points.
	
#Skills#
You have +1 in two Skills of choice

#Stats#
Base Health: 10
Movement: 6 meters
Lifespan: ~80 years
Languages: Common

#Language#
If your Intelligence is at least 1, you know one additional Common language.

#OTHER#
You start with 1 extra Feat Point.

#Abilities#
You start with the following racial ability:

[Human Vigilence] : Passive
Whenever you make a Skill Check, instead of rolling, you can automatically have 7 + your skill modifiers for that check.


#LevelBonus#
As a Human, a Level 1 you can pick a Talent from any other race:
	
{Human Diversity} : Special
Choose any other race.
Gain the first talent (left-most, first row, aka Level 1 Race Talent) from that race (except Dragonborn).

#LevelBonus#
At Level 3, your maximum number of Charges increases by +1.
Your maximum Health also increses by 1 + Your Fortitude, in addition to the maximum Health you usually gain when leveling!
Also, pick one of the following Talents and know it permanently:


#PageEnd#

	At Level 3:
{Master of the World} : Passive
Increase any stat by 1 (up to +3).





{Negotiation Skills} : Passive
You get a 10% discount on everything you buy and don't bargain for.
