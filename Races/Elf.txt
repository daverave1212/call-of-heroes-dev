#Race#
	Elf
A race in tune with both nature and magic, the elves highly praise intelligence and knowledge, as well as 'refined' arts of combat.

#Options#
When creating your character:
	a) Either increase two stats (except Fortitude) by 1, or increase Fortitude by 1.
	b) You have +1 in Dexterity saves and Intelligence saves.
	
#Skills#
You have +1 Nature, +1 Knowledge, +1 Arcana
You have -1 Athletics, -1 Use Rope, -1 Luck

#Stats#
Base Health: 10
Movement: 6 meters
Lifespan: ~200 years
Languages: Common and Elvish

#Weapons#
You are trained in Longbows and Longswords.

#Abilities#
You start with the following racial ability:

[The Blade Dance] : Passive
When you land a critical strike, you reset your movement this turn and don't provoke attacks of oportunity this turn.

#LevelBonus#
At Level 1, you can pick one of the following talents and know it permanently:
	
{Meditation} : 4 Hours
Instead of sleeping for 8 hours, you can meditate for 4 hours and achieve the same result.
While meditating, all checks you perform are done at -5.	

{Arcane Veins} : Passive
You permanently know 2 spells from the Amateur Spell List.

{Darkvision} : Passive
You can see up to 12 meters away in darkness, without discerning colors.

#LevelBonus#
At Level 3, pick one of the following Talents and know it permanently:

{Mana Tap} : 0 Actions
Spend 1 Charge.
An enemy near you rolls an Intelligence save.
If it fails, it is Silenced.
	
{Natual Swiftness} : 0 Actions
Gain +4 Movement this turn and you avoid Attacks of Oportunity.
You can use this once per Long Rest.

{Landsperson} : Passive
Choose any land specialty ability from Coven of the Land Druid specialization and learn it permanently.


#PageEnd#

	At Level 3:



{Graceful Evasion} : Reaction
When you are attacked, before the attack roll, you gain +3 Armor against that attack.
You can use this once per Long Rest.

{Elven Discipline} : Passive
You have Advantage against being Charmed or Feared.

	Feat points

	Dark Vision
	Number of Maneuvers
