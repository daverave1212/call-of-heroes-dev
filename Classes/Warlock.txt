
#Class#
		Warlock
Warlocks are people who made pacts with powerful beings

#Options#
When creating your character:
	a) Choose 2 of these, and increase them by 1: Charisma, Intelligence, Wisdom, Dexterity, Fortitude
	b) You have +2 in Fortitude Saves and Charisma Saves.	

#Skills#
You have +1 Religion, +1 Knowledge and +1 Perception.
You have -1 Stealth, -1 Luck and -1 Survival

#Stats#
Base Health: 9 + Race Health + 2 * Fortitude


#Language#
If your Intelligence is at least 2, choose one Higher language you can speak.

#LevelUp#
Every level, you gain +5 Health, 1 Skill Point and 1 extra known Spell or Maneuver.

At levels 2, 4, 6, 8 and 10, you gain a Talent from your chosen specialization.
At levels 3, 5, 7 and 9 you gain 1 extra Charge and a Talent from your race.


#MainStat#
Choose between Intelligence or Charisma. This is your Main Stat.

Your Spell DC is 10 + (Main Stat).
Your Spell Hit Bonus is (Main Stat).

You have 2 Charges: O O
You regain 1 Charge back after every worthy Combat encounter.

All Warlock-specific abilities are considered spells.

You can change your known Spells (not Talents) when leveling up.

When creating your character, choose one type of being you serve. That being is called your Patron:
- Fey Patron, a wild, outworldly being, heavily magical or in tune with nature, and often outward strange. A Fey Patron often represents an old god of nature or magic, resembled by a magical animal or humanoid.
- Celestial Patron, a being from a divine plain. Portrayed as angels or divine spirits of life and death
- Demon or Devil patron, a being residing in Hell, an archdevil or lord in hell
- Old God Patron, an eldritch being of nightmare and terror, whose thoughts, form and wants are incomprehensible to most people

[Summon Familiar] : 1 Hour	 //	~ 1 Charge
You conjure a ritual using various magical materials worth 25 Gold.
The first time you cast this ritual, you summon a familiar which becomes yours.
You can choose which type of familiar you want:
- It can take the shape of a small creature.
- It can look spectral or look as a normal animal with a slight glow (does not radiate light).
- You can choose what color it is if it's spectral or what color its glow is.
Also, choose an element which is bound to it: Fire, Water, Frost, Arcane, Thunder, Earth, Wind, Light or Darkness.
That becomes your Familiar Element.
Your familiar comes with a name. Roll for your familiar name on the Familiar Name Table (or your DM will choose one for you).
You can communicate with your Familiar telepathically.
The familiar doesn't take turns on its own, but at the same time as you.
On your turn, your familiar can use its own movement to move, but you must spend your actions for it to take actions.
If your familiar goes more than 20 meters away from you, it becomes unsummoned.
If it dies, it becomes unsummoned.
You can always summon it back with this Ability.
After your familiar is unsummoned, it needs 24 hours to be summonable back.
Your familiar has the following stats:
- Health: 8 + Your Fortitude + 2 * Your Level.
- Defense: Your Dexterity
- Stats and Saves: Same as yours
- Movement: Same as yours, can't fly, but it can swim

[Pet Attack] : 0.5 Actions
The pet melee attacks a target.
The attack deals 1d10 + (Your Main Stat) damage (of your pet's elemental type).
You can do this once per turn.
	
As a summoner, you have access to special Familiar Ability.
You can unlock one Familiar Ability for 2 Skill Points.
These feats give extra abilities for your Familiar.
They are considered Maneuvers for you and your pet.
When creating your character, you also get one of these Abilities for free.


	[Element Lash] : 1 Action
	Range: 3 meters (from your Familiar)
	The closest enemy (you choose if tie) to the familiar makes a Dexterity save.
	If it fails, it takes 2d12 damage (of your Familiar Element).
	
	[Soul Attack] : 1 Action
	Cost: 1 Charge.
	Range: 1 meter (from your Familiar)
	A creature near your familiar makes a Dexterity save.
	It takes 6d4 + Main Stat damage if it fails, or half that much if it succeeds.
	After that, your familiar briefly disappears.
	It appears back near you at the start of your next-next turn.
	
	[Meat Shield] : Reaction
	Passively, your pet doubles its maximum health and its size becomes Large.
	When you are attacked, before the attack roll, you can activate this to make your pet dash to your position and the attack will be redirected to your pet.
	1 use / Long Rest.
	
	[Sentience] : Passive
	Your familiar can speak and communicate in all languages you know.
	Your Spell DC also increases by 1 and your Maximum Health by 3.
	
	[Seduce] : 0.5 Actions
	Cost: 1 Charge
	Range: 6 meters (from your Familiar)
	A target rolls a Wisdom save
	If it fails, it is Charmed by your familiar, and your familiar is also Charmed by that creature.
	(Nothing happens if the target is immune to being Charmed).
	
	[Devour Magic] : 1 Action
	Cost: 1 Charge.
	Cure a debuff, curse or poison on an ally.
	Also, choose a target near the familiar.
	That target rolls an Intelligence save.
	If it fails, it is Silenced and takes equal to its rolled Save (of your Familiar Element).

	[Health Funnel] : 0 Actions
	Deal 6 damage to your Familiar, and you gain 3 Health.
	Or..
	Deal 6 damage to yourself, and your Familiar gains 3 Health.
	
	[Camouflage Master] : 1 Action
	Cost: 1 Charge.
	You and your familiar gain +5 Stealth for 10 minutes.
