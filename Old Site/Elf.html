    Elf

    A race in tune with both nature and magic, the elves highly praise intelligence and knowledge, as well as 'refined' arts of combat.


    Character Setup


    Stats and Saves

    Choose 2 of the following stats and increase them by 1...

    • Charisma
    • Intelligence
    • Wisdom
    • Dexterity

    Or choose 1 of these and increase it by 1:

    • Fortitude

    As a Elf, you have the following:

    • +1 Dexterity Save
    • +1 Intelligence Save

    You also have:

    • 1 Nature
    • 1 Knowledge
    • 1 Arcana
    • -1 Athletics
    • -1 Use Rope
    • -1 Luck

    Your Speed is 6 meters meters.

    Your starting Health is 9.

    Your total Health will be Class Health + 9 + 2 * Fortitude + (5 for each level after Level 0).


    Other Things

    By default, you speak Common and Elvish

    People of your kind live for 200 years and their size is medium


    Starting Abilities

    The Blade Dance (Passive)

    When you land the killing strike on a Worthy Enemy, you reset your movement this turn and don't provoke attacks of oportunity this turn.



    When creating your character, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


    Meditation (4 Hours)

    Instead of sleeping for 8 hours, you can meditate for 4 hours and achieve the same result.
    While meditating, all checks you perform are done at -5.

    Arcane Veins (Passive)

    You permanently know 2 spells from the Amateur Spell List.

    Darkvision (Passive)

    You can see up to 12 meters away in darkness, without discerning colors.
