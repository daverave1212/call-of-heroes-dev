    Animals and Pets


    Bear

    Health 10 + Fortitude * 2 + 4 * Level
    Stats 5/1/0/2/2

    ~Maul~ (1 Action)

    Long Rest

    the Bear tries to Stun a creature near it (Fortitude save).

    ~Swiping Claw~ (1 Action)

    All creatures around the Bear take 6 damage (slash).


    Cat

    Health 10 + 3 * Level
    Stats 1/5/0/2/2

    ~Prowl~ (Passive)

    The Cat and it's owner leave no tracks.
    The Cat and it's owner have +2 to Stealth rolls.

    ~Rake~ (0.5 Actions)

    Long Rest

    The Cat tries to Cripple a creature near it (Dexterity save).
    Does not work on creatures that can't bleed.


    Bird of Prey

    Health 5 + 3 * Level
    Stats 0/5/0/2/2
    Defense (at least) 5

    ~Talons~ (Passive)

    An enemy starting its turn near the Bird of Prey is slowed by 50%.

    ~Beak Poke~ (1 Action)

    A creature near the Bird of Prey rolls a Dexterity save.
    If it fails, it is Blinded and takes 1d8 damage.


    Wolf

    Health 10 + 3 * Level
    Stats 2/3/0/4/3
    Movement Speed +2 meters

    ~Ferocity~ (Passive)

    Pet attack deals 1d8 instead of 1d6 damage.


    If you are a druid, your first attack every turn does +1 damage.

    ~Leap~ (0.5 Actions)

    Exactly 3 meters range, no more, no less.

    The Wolf leaps at a target (near it).
    The target rolls a Dexterity save.
    If it fails, the target is Knocked Prone.


    Deer

    Health 10 + 3 * Level
    Stats 1/3/0/4/0
    Movement Speed +4 meters out of combat

    ~Rideable~ (Passive)

    You (or an ally) can ride your Deer as if you were Trained in riding.

    ~Majestic Buffet~ (0.5 Actions)

    Long Rest

    Use this after you used Pet Attack.
    The same target makes a Charisma save.
    If it fails, it is Charmed (by the Deer).


    If you are a Druid, use this after you performed a melee attack.


    Giant Spider

    Health 10 + 3 * Level
    Stats 1/2/0/1/2

    ~Poison Fangs~ (Passive)

    Giant Spider attacks deal +1d6 extra Poison damage to targets affected by Crowd Control.

    ~Web~ (0.5 Actions)

    exactly 3 to 6 meters

    The Giant Spider tries to Snare a creature (Dexterity save).
