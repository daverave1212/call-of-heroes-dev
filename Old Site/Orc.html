    Orc

    Tribal in nature, though not stupid.

    Orc societies are highly based on honor and strength.

    Many orcs are also in tune with nature or spirits, allowing them to be both powerful and cunning.

    Unlike orcs in traditional fantasy, Orcs in Call of Heroes are not necessarily vile and reckless.

    Orcs are leaders and hunters by nature, but will also be lead by someone stronger or who can indeed lead better than them.

    Even in a more peaceful tribe, the most charismatic, witty and strong orc will be the leader.

    Orcs are also driven by control (over territory, goods, etc).

    Many of them wish to be in control - they don't like other people doing things for them, unless they are sure they can do it better.

    For this reason, many orcs make a living without help.

    They build their own houses, hunt their own food or pillage for both the good of the tribe and their own.


    Character Setup


    Stats and Saves

    Choose 2 of the following stats and increase them by 1...

    • Charisma
    • Wisdom
    • Dexterity
    • Fortitude

    Or choose 1 of these and increase it by 1:

    • Intelligence

    As a Orc, you have the following:

    • +1 Fortitude Save

    You also have:

    • 1 Athletics
    • 1 Monstrology
    • 1 Intimidation
    • -1 Knowledge
    • -1 Arcana
    • -1 Persuasion

    Your Speed is 6 meters meters.

    Your starting Health is 11.

    Your total Health will be Class Health + 11 + 2 * Fortitude + (5 for each level after Level 0).


    Other Things

    By default, you speak Common and Orcish

    If your Intelligence is at least 2, you can speak an additional Wild Language.

    People of your kind live for 80 years and their size is Normal


    You start with 1 extra known maneuver.


    Starting Abilities

    Boiling Blood (0 Actions)

    Long Rest

    Lose half of your current health.
    Increase the original damage of your next attack by 50%.


    Extra abilities over that attack are not increased.

    Battle Anguish (Negative Passive)

    Whevener you drop to 0 Health, your maximum Health permanently decreases by 1.



    When creating your character, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


    Fierceness (Passive)

    You can use any weapon with Fortitude (instead of Dexterity, in some cases).

    Adaptability (Passive)

    You can have your Fortitude as your Main Stat, even if your class says otherwise.

    Beast Rider (Passive)

    You are trained in Riding and Exotic Riding (you can ride exotic mounts, such as wolves).
    You also have such an exotic mount of your choice, if the DM accepts it.
    You gain +2 in Animal Handling.


    This mount is a non-combat pet. It does not participate in combat.

    Darkvision (Passive)

    You can see up to 12 meters away in darkness, without discerning colors.
