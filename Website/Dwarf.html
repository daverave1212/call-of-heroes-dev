    Dwarf

    A race of old, the ancient Dwarves are a strong and traditional people.


    Character Setup


    Stats and Saves

    Choose 2 of the following stats and increase them by 1...

    • Charisma
    • Intelligence
    • Wisdom
    • Fortitude

    Or choose 1 of these and increase it by 1:

    • Dexterity

    As a Dwarf, you have the following:

    • +1 Fortitude Save

    You also have:

    • 1 Athletics
    • 1 History
    • 1 Craft
    • -1 Acrobatics
    • -1 Deception
    • -1 Stealth

    Your Speed is 5 meters meters.

    Your starting Health is 10.

    Your total Health will be Class Health + 10 + 2 * Fortitude + (5 for each level after Level 0).


    Other Things

    By default, you speak Common and Dwarvish

    People of your kind live for 250 years and their size is medium


    Starting Abilities

    Dwarf Resting (Passive)

    You need 12 hours of sleep instead of 8 per day to function normally.
    Your movement speed can't be reduced below 5 meters.


    Hard Terrain still takes 2 meters of movement for 1 meter of Hard Terrain.

    Dwarven Resilience (Reaction)

    Reroll a save roll.
    You can do this once per Long Rest.



    When creating your character, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


    Stone-like Bones (Passive)

    Your bones are tough as rock.
    You take half as much damage from falling and from traps which deal physical damage.
    You also get +1 on Fortitude saves.

    Darkvision (Passive)

    You can see up to 12 meters away in darkness, without discerning colors.

    Runesmith (Passive)

    You permanently know 1 spell from the Amateur Spell List.
    You can identify what language most texts are written in by analyzing them.
    Your Arcana skill increases by 1.
