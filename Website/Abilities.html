    Abilities

    Maneuvers

    Improvised Attack (0.5 or 1 Action)

    You can use small, medium or large non-weapon objects from around and attack with them.
    Small Objects - 0.5 Actions, 1d10 + Dexterity damage, thrown at 3 to 6 meters range
    Medium Objects - 0.5 Actions, 1d10 + Fortitude damage, 2 meters range
    Large Objects - 1 Action, 2d10 + 3 damage, 1 meter range
    If you roll 10 on any of those dice and the object can break, it breaks and deals an extra 1d6 damage.

    Dash (1 Action)

    You can move 4 more meters this turn.

    Hide (Reaction)

    When you would roll Initiative, you can instead roll Stealth.
    If you succeed, you become Hidden.
    While Hidden, you can unhide and start your turn whenever you like (even before or after a creature does something).
    If you started your turn during another creature's turn, that creature resumes its turn after your turn ends.
    After that, you take your turn when you normally would, next rounds.


    You become unhidden at the end of the round if you don't take your turn by then. The Stealth check is made againt the highest enemy Passive Perception, which is their Perception + 10.

    Defend (1 Action)

    Until the start of your next turn, your Defense and all saves increase by +2.

    Push (1 Action)

    1 meter

    Try to push a creature 1 meter in any direction (Fortitude or Dexterity save, its choice).


    You can always Push even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the save.

    Shift (0 Actions)

    You can move 1 meter without triggering attacks of oportunity.
    This turn you can't move normally (and you can't use this if you already did).

    Bull Rush (Passive)

    If you move at least 3 meters in a straight line and then melee attack a target, the attack gains +1.

    Taunt (0.5 Actions)

    6 meters

    Make an enemy roll a Wisdom save.
    If it fails, its next attack gains Cover (-2) if it's not on you.


    You can always Taunt even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the save.

    Grapple (1 Action)

    You make a Fortitude roll and the target makes a Dexterity or Fortitude roll (its choice).
    If you roll equal to or more than the enemy, the enemy becomes Grappled.


    A Grappled enemy can't move on its turn, has 0.5 fewer Actions and can't attack you. You and the enemy repeat the roll at the end of each of that enemy's turn. On your turn, you must spend 1 Action to keep the enemy Grappled. If you don't, the enemy is released. You can always Grapple even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the roll.

    Hamstring (0.5 Actions)

    1 meter

    Try to Slow a target (Dexterity save).

    Disengage (1 Action)

    You don't trigger attacks of oportunity this turn.

    Aim (0.5 Actions)

    Your next attack has Advantage (lasts at most until the end of your next turn).

    Lightweight Strike (0.5 Actions)

    Make an attack with a 2-Handed weapon with Disadvantage.
    After the attack, your turn ends immediately.

    Wait (0 Actions)

    1 turn

    You start your turn later this Encounter at that initiative.


    Your new initiative stays like that for later turns.

    Shank (0 Actions)

    Double Greatweapon Fighting (Passive)

    Your Defense is reduced by 1 wielding weapons like this

    You can dual wield 2-Handed melee weapons, one in each hand.
    If you attack like this, each attack takes only 0.5 Actions.
    Your attacks like this do Weapon Damage + Fortitude or Dexterity (depending on weapon).
    When dual wielding them, if any ability tells you to perform an attack with a 2-Handed and the ability costs 1 Action, it costs only 0.5 Actions.


    Alternatively, you can wield a Shield in one hand and a 2-Handed melee weapon in the other. The Defense penalty still applies. You need Training in Shields to do this.


    Requires Trained in 2-Handed melee weapons and at least 2 Fortitude

    Highlander Technique (Passive)

    You can wield 1-Handed melee weapons with both hands.
    If you do, your 2-Handed attacks with 1-Handed melee weapons take 1 Action and apply 2 * Fortitude or Dexterity (depending on the weapon).
    While doing this, you have +1 to all Saves.


    If an Ability tells you to make an attack with a 2-Handed weapon, attack with the 2 * Fortitude or Dexterity bonus If an Ability tells you to make an attack with a 1-Handed weapon, attack with the normal Fortitude or Dexterity bonus


    Requires Trained in 1-Handed and 2-Handed melee weapons

    Duelist Technique (Passive)

    If you are wielding a 1-Handed melee weapon in one hand and nothing in the other, you can attack twice per turn with that weapon without penalty.


    Normally, you can only attack normally once with each hand.


    Requires Trained in 1-Handed melee weapons and Finesse weapons

    You can wield a Tower Shield.
    A Tower Shield gives you a +2 Defense bonus, but reduces your Movement Speed by 2.


    Check the Shop to see the price of a Tower Shield.

    Control Water (1 Action)

    6 meters

    Choose a 1x1x1 meter cube of water or ice.
    You can do the following things to it.
    - You can move it 1 meter in any direction

    At level 8, you can evaporate or freeze 1x1x1 meter cube of water with 1 Action.

    Effects last up to 1 hour.

    Control Earth (1 Action)

    6 meters

    Choose a 1x1x1 meter cube of dirt, loose stone, sand, etc.
    You can do the following things to it.

    If it fails, it takes 1d8 damage and is knocked prone.
    If a creature is burried in dirt (or loose stone, etc) after failing the save, it takes 1d8 additional damage.
    - You can roughly shape it in any form
    - You can cause it to become difficult terrain.
    You can do this twice per Long Rest at level 1, 3 times per Long Rest at level 4 and unlimited times at level 8.

    1 Charge.

    Touch a slightly broken piece of material that is still in one piece.
    You can repair the damage done to it.


    For example, repair a hole through a chain shirt or textile material, the crack in a piece of glass, etc.

    Spare the Dying (1 Action)

    1 Charge

    Touch a creature that died at most 10 minutes ago or an unconscious creature.
    If the creature is dead, and if the creature's body is fairly intact, the dead creature comes back to life, stabilized and unconscious.
    If the creature is unconscious, the creature wakes up and is healed for 1 Health.
