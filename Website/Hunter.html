    Hunter

    You are a hunter, tracker, always on your toes. No matter your foe, be it a beast, a vampire or a dragon, you know best how to deal with it.


    Character Setup


    Stats and Saves

    Choose 2 of the following stats and increase them by 1:

    • Wisdom
    • Dexterity
    • Fortitude

    As a Hunter, you have the following:

    • +3 Dexterity Saves

    You also have:

    • 1 Nature
    • 1 Stealth
    • 1 Perception
    • -1 Religion
    • -1 History
    • -1 Performance


    Your starting Health is 6.

    Your total Health will be 6 + Race Health + 2 * Fortitude + (5 for each level after Level 0).


    When Leveling Up...

    Whenever you gain a Level after Level 0, you gain:

    • +5 Health
    • +1 Skill Point
    • +1 extra known Spell or Maneuver
    • +1 extra maximum Charge

    Other Things

    If your Intelligence is at least 1, choose one Common or Wild language you can speak.

    Wicked Slayer (Passive)

    Your weapon attacks and thrown spells against Monstrosities, Celestials and Unholy creatures have +1.
    Your attacks are always treated as Magical or Silver for the purpose of some effects.

    Mage Slayer (Reaction)

    1 Charge

    Use when an enemy casts a spell.
    Try to Silence it and prevent that spell (Wisdom save).

    Giant Slayer (Passive)

    Weapon attacks and thrown spells deal +1 damage against Large or larger targets.
    Enemies don't gain flanking bonus when attacking you.

    Manking Slayer (Passive)

    Against Humanoids, Slay both deals the extra damage and tries to Snare it, no matter the health.


    Spells and Abilities

    Choose between Wisdom or Charisma. This is your Main Stat.

    Spell DC is 10 + Main Stat.

    You start with 3 Charges.

    You regain all Charges back when you Long Rest.

    You can change your known Spells (not Talents) when taking a long rest outdoors.

    Number of known maneuvers: 1 + Your Intelligence

    Number of known spells: Your Level

    Starting Abilities

    Slay (0 Actions)

    1 Charge

    Your next attack has the following effect:
    If the target has 50% of less Health, deal 1d12 extra damage.
    If it has more, try to Snare it (Wisdom save).

    Tumble (0 Actions)

    Long Rest

    Move 1 meter in any direction, without triggering attacks of oportunity.



    Specializations

    At Level 1, you can pick your class Specialization. This will define the way your character's class is unique, as it offers you a choice for which path to take and follow for the rest of your journey. Every 2 levels (2, 4, 6, 8, 10) check back here and see what special abilities you unlock through your Specialization. Choose wisely!


    • Draven, a tracker and hunter of beats and men, never lost and never without purpose
    • Warden, a potent user of fast and practical magic to use against foes, and with allies
    • Beastmaster, a pack hunter, who finds friends in even the most ruthless of beasts

    Draven

    lorem


    You start with the following Draven abilities:


    Tracker (Passive)

    o You can more easily track creatures.
    When you try to track a creature, your roll for that try gains +3.
    If you succeed, you may learn about its size, how many of them there are, if it's injured or roughly how far away it is.
    o Your party can't get lost in the wild except by magical means
    o You (and your pets) are unaffected by Rough Terrain.

    Hunter's Mark (0 Actions)

    1 Charge

    12 meters

    Long Rest

    Choose and mark a target.
    Your single target attacks and thrown spells against the marked target have the damage dice increased by 1 tier.
    When the target with the mark dies, you can use your Reaction to freely move the mark to another enemy.


    Ex: instead of 2d6, an attack would be 2d8. D12's don't increase, they stay d12.


    Talents

    At Level 2, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


    Animal Companion (Passive)

    You have an animal pet that can fight alongside you!
    Your animal has stats depending on its species.
    By default, apply the following stats to your pet (these will get modified by your pet's species):
    - Health: Half of yours
    - Movement: 6 meters
    Your pet takes its turn at the same time as you do, and always has the same initiative as yours.
    Your pet can make normal Skill Checks using your Skill bonuses, or its own, using its special Stats.
    Your pet will not stray more than 30 meters away from you in hostile territory.
    On your turn, you can command it to move, but it can't take its own Actions.
    You and your pet share the same Actions on a turn.
    (ex: If you have 1 Action, your pet can use 0.5 Actions to attack and you can use 0.5 Actions to attack too, or your pet spends 1 Action and you skip your Actions, etc.)
    All pets have the Pet Attack ability:
    : 0.5 Actions
    The pet melee attacks a target.
    The attack has + your Main Stat.
    The attack deals 1d6 + your Main Stat - 1 (can't be lower than 1d6 - 1).
    You can do this once per turn.
    Some abilities may say 'Perform a Pet Attack'. In that case, ignore the once-per-turn.

    Flare Shot (Reaction or 0 Actions)

    1 Charge

    15 meters

    Quickly shoot a flare at a target point.
    The area around the flare lights up in a 6 meter radius, creating normal light.
    When cast, all allies in the area are cleared of Stun, Fear or Cripple.
    Allies in the area have Advantage on rolls against Fear.
    The flare lasts 1 minute.

    Night Senses (0 Actions)

    1 Charge

    For 1 Hour, you gain the following:
    Your party has Dark Vision (15 meter range).
    Your party has Advantage on rolls against Blinding.
    Your party has +2 meter movement speed.


    Warden

    Lorem :)


    You start with the following Warden abilities:


    Warden (Passive)

    When creating your character, you can choose 2 more spells to know permanently from the Mage or Cleric spells list.
    You also gain +1 in Arcana, Luck and Knowledge, and your Spell DC is increased by 2.
    Slay is replaced by Quick Magic. Quick Magic is considered a Spell.

    Quick Magic (0 Actions)

    1 or more Charges

    Turn

    Spend any number of Charges.
    Choose one of the following effects, then apply the exact same effect for each Charge spent:
    - Instantly deal 1d6 Fire, Frost or Shock damage to a target, and permanently reduce its Armor by 1 (3 meter range).
    - Instantly heal a target for 1d6 (3 meter range).
    - Instantly dash 3 meters, ignoring attacks of oportunity, and going through enemies and small obstacles or medium gaps.


    You can do this once per turn.

    Watcher Form (1 Action)

    1 Charge

    Long Rest

    You take on a special form, called a Watcher Form.
    Your body becomes engulfed in a magical aura (you choose its color), your eyes glow, and you can gain other aesthetics-only body transformations (customize it with your DM).
    While in this form, your first weapon attack (or thrown spell) deals +4 damage once per turn, you gain +2 to all saves and +2 to your Save DC and +3 to Intimidation and Arcana.
    Also, when you cast a spell (before you do), you can instantly dash 3 meters, ignoring attacks of oportunity, and going through enemies and small obstacles or medium gaps.
    Lasts up to 1 minute.

    Combo Set (Passive)

    You gain Quickfire, Guard Breaker and Tumblestrike.

    Quickfire (0 Actions)

    1 Charge

    Perform an attack with a (loaded if it has to be) 1-Handed weapon.
    This attack does not trigger attacks of oportunity and can be used in melee range without penalty if it's a ranged weapon.

    Guard Breaker (Passive)

    Your Slash, Smash and Pierce attacks ignore target's resistances to that damage type (not immunity, however).

    Tumblestrike (Passive)

    After you Tumble, your next weapon attack or thrown spell has Advantage on the damage roll.

    At Level 2, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:

    You have one more Animal Companion, aside from the one you get from your Beastmaster Companion.
    This Animal Companion obeys the same rules as the regular one.
    On a turn, you can do one Pet Attack for each pet.

    Exotic Trainer (Passive)
