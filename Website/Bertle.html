    Bertle

    Bertles are rabbit people.


    Character Setup


    Stats and Saves

    Choose 2 of the following stats and increase them by 1...

    • Charisma
    • Intelligence
    • Wisdom
    • Dexterity

    Or choose 1 of these and increase it by 1:

    • Fortitude

    As a Bertle, you have the following:

    • +1 Dexterity Saves Save

    You also have:

    • 1 Acrobatics
    • 1 Perception
    • 1 Craft
    • -1 Animal Handling
    • -1 Deception
    • -1 History

    Your Speed is 7 meters meters.

    Your starting Health is 9.

    Your total Health will be Class Health + 9 + 2 * Fortitude + (5 for each level after Level 0).


    Other Things

    By default, you speak Common

    If your Intelligence is at least 2, you can speak 1 additional Common language.

    People of your kind live for 75 years and their size is Small or Medium (your choice)


    Due to the variations in Bertle species, you can choose if you want your size to be Small or Medium.


    Starting Abilities

    Herbivore (Passive)

    You can not eat meat.
    If you do, you take take 1d12 Poison damage and have Disadvantage on all rolls for 1 hour.
    You have 0 Defense against Poison attacks.



    When creating your character, choose one of the following abilities and gain it permanently:


    Fleet Footed (Passive)

    You are immune to Slows (not counting Hard Terrain).

    Nimbleness (Passive)

    You can move through the spaces of enemy units (in normal conditions).

    Darkvision (Passive)

    You can see up to 12 meters away in darkness, without discerning colors.

    Cutting Teeth (Passive)

    Your teeth act as natural daggers which can pierce through some materials:
    You are able to cut rope, flesh, wood, etc (but not concrete, metal, etc).
    Attacking with your teeth takes 0.5 Action, adds Dexterity modifier and deals 1d8 + 1 Piercing damage.
    You can only bite once per turn.
