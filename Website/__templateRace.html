Dragonborn

Born of dragons, as their name proclaims, the dragonborn walk proudly through a world that greets them with fearful incomprehension. Shaped by draconic gods or the dragons themselves, dragonborn originally hatched from dragon eggs as a unique race, combining the best attributes of dragons and humanoids.


Character Setup


Stats and Saves

Choose 2 of the following stats and increase them by 1...

  • Fortitude
  • Wisdom
  • Intelligence
  • Charisma

Or choose only 1 of these and increase it by 1

  • Dexterity

As a Dragonborn, you have the following:

  • +1 Fortitude Saves
  • +1 Intelligence Saves

You also have:

  • +1 Religion
  • +1 Luck
  • +1 Persuasion
  • -1 Biology
  • -1 Investigation
  • -1 Deception

Your Speed is 6 meters.

Your starting Health is 11.

Your total Health will be Class Health + 11 + 2 * Fortitude + (5 for each level after Level 1).


Other Things

You can speak Common.

If your Intelligence is at least 2 (after finishing up your character), choose one Higher or Common language you can speak.


Dragonborn live for around 80 years and are medium sized creatures.

Dragonborn have tails.
