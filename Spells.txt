	Maneuvers: =<

[Improvised Attack] : 0.5 or 1 Action
You can use small, medium or large non-weapon objects from around and attack with them.
- Small Objects: 0.5 Actions, 1d10 + Dexterity damage, thrown at 3 to 6 meters range
- Medium Objects: 0.5 Actions, 1d10 + Fortitude damage, 2 meters range
- Large Objects: 1 Action, 2d10 + 3 damage, 1 meter range
If you roll 10 on any of those dice and the object can break, it breaks and deals an extra 1d10 damage.

[Dash] : 1 Action
You can move 4 more meters this turn.

[Hide] : Reaction
When you roll for Initiative and the enemies don't see you, you can become Hidden.
While Hidden, you can unhide and start your turn whenever you like (even before or after a creature does something).
If you started your turn during another creature's turn, that creature resumes its turn after your turn ends.
After that, you take your turn when you normally would, next rounds.
_You become unhidden at the end of the round if you don't take your turn by then._

[Defend] : 1 Action
Until the start of your next turn, your Defense and all saves increase by +2.	

[Push] : 1 Action
Range: 1 meter
A creature makes a Fortitude or Dexterity save (its choice).
If it fails, it is pushed 1 meter away from you (in any direction).
_You can always Push even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the save._

[Shift] : 0 Actions
You can move 1 meter without triggering attacks of oportunity.
This turn you can't move normally (and you can't use this if you already did).

[Bull Rush] : Passive
If you move at least 3 meters in a straight line and then melee attack a target, the attack gains +1.

[Taunt] : 0.5 Actions
Range: 6 meters
Make an enemy roll a Wisdom save.
If it fails, its next attack will (most probably) be on you.
_You can always Taunt even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the save._

[Grapple] : 1 Action
Attempt to grapple an enemy.
You make a Fortitude roll and the enemy makes a Dexterity or Fortitude roll (its choice).
If you roll equal to or more than the enemy, the enemy becomes Grappled.
A Grappled enemy can't move on its turn, has 0.5 fewer Actions and can't attack you.
You and the enemy repeat the roll at the end of each of that enemy's turn.
On your turn, you must spend 1 Action to keep the enemy Grappled. If you don't, the enemy is released.
_You can always Grapple even if you don't have this maneuver, but the target has Advantage on the roll._

[Hamstring] : 0.5 Actions
Range: 1 meter
A creature makes a Dexterity save.
If it fails, its maximum speed becomes 3 meters.

[Disengage] : 1 Action
You don't trigger attacks of oportunity this turn.

[Aim] : 0.5 Actions
This turn, your next attack which takes 0.5 Actions has Advantage on the hit roll.

[Wait] : 1 Action
Increase or decrease your initiative by 5 this encounter.

[Overthrow] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
A creature near you makes a Fortitude save and you make a Fortitude check.
If you roll more than that creature's save, deal 3d4 + 2 damage to it and throw the creature over your shoulder, on the opposite side of your position, next to you.
	
[Shank] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge
Make a melee attack with your off-hand weapon. It has -2 to hit.
	
[Double Greatweapon Fighting] : Passive
Requirement: Skilled in 2-Handed melee weapons and at least 2 Fortitude
Cost: Your Defense is reduced by 1 if you have this Maneuver
You can dual wield 2-Handed melee weapons, one in each hand.
If you attack like this, each attack takes only 0.5 Actions.
Your attacks like this do Weapon Damage + Fortitude or Dexterity (depending on weapon).
When dual wielding them, if any ability tells you to perform an attack with a 2-Handed and the ability costs 1 Action, it costs only 0.5 Actions.

	Elemental =<
[Acid Burst] : 0.5 Actions
Range: 3 meters
A creature makes a Dexterity save.
It takes 1d8 + 1 Acid damage on a failed save.
_Variant: Poison Burst.

[Burning Breath] : 0.5 Actions
All creatures in a 90 degree, 3 meter cone take 3 Fire damage.

[Bonfire] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
Duration: 10 minutes
Choose a 1x1x1 meter zone.
A bonfire errupts in that place.
Any creature touching the bonfire takes 1d8 Fire damage.
_If the bonfire can spread fire to nearby flammable objects that aren't being worn or carried, it spreads at the start of your next turn (same for further bonfires)._

[Control Flames] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
Choose a 1x1x1 meter cube of fire.
You can do the following things to it:
- You can spread the fire 1 meter in any direction
- You can extinguish it
- You can double of halve the radius of light or heat it spreads
- You can change its color
- You can cause simple shapes to appear in the flicker.
You can have 2 effects active at the same time at level 1, 3 at level 4 and 4 at level 8.
Effects last up to 1 hour.
If the fire touches a creature, it takes 1d8 Fire damage.

[Control Water] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
Choose a 1x1x1 meter cube of water or ice.
You can do the following things to it:
- You can move it 1 meter in any direction
- You can roughly alter its shape
- You can change its color or opacity
- You can freeze or evaporate the water. This will take up to 5 minutes at level 1 for 1x1x1 meter cube of water.
At level 8, you can evaporate or freeze 1x1x1 meter cube of water with 1 Action.
The water does no damage if it touches a creature.
Effects last up to 1 hour.

[Control Earth] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
Choose a 1x1x1 meter cube of dirt, loose stone, sand, etc.
You can do the following things to it:
- You can move it 1 meter in any direction.
If a creature was hit by it or it was removed from below a creature's feet, it rolls a Dexterity save (against your Spell DC).
If it fails, it takes 1d8 damage and is knocked prone.
If a creature is burried in dirt (or loose stone, etc) after failing the save, it takes 1d8 additional damage.
- You can roughly shape it in any form
- You can cause it to become difficult terrain.
You can do this twice per Long Rest at level 1, 3 times per Long Rest at level 4 and unlimited times at level 8.

[Fire Bolt] : 1 Action
Range: 3 meters
Make a Spell attack.
If you hit, deal 3d8 Fire damage.
_Variant: Inflict Wounds, Necrotic damage._

[Frostbite] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
A creature makes a Fortitude save and takes 2d8 + 2 damage if it fails, or half if it succeeds.
If it fails, it is also Slowed.

[Land Breach] : 1 Actions
Range: 9 meters
You gather stone, dirt, sand, etc from the surroundings and release it upon an enemy.
Deal 3d6 damage Force damage to a creature (against Defense), and two of rolled dice (you choose) to its adjacent creatures (against Defense).

[Magic Missiles] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
Shoot 3 missiles. Each missile does 1d4.
At Level 4, you can also spend any number of charges, and you shoot 2 more missiles for each Charge spent.
You can send missiles to different targets, but you must declare all the attacks at once.
_Each missile can be affected by Cover and Hard Cover._

[Quicksand] : 0.5 Actions
Range: 6 meters to center
Duration: 1 minute
Declare a 2x2 meter zone made of dirt, sand or other such material.
That zone becomes Difficult Terrain. Any creature on it is Slowed while it's on the zone.
_Variant: You can also cause this difficult terrain with vegetation._

[Thunder Clap] : 1 Action
Attack all creatures near you for 2d8 Electric damage (against their Defense).

[Crackling Lightining] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 6 meters
Duration: Until the target dies or you don't do this
Declare a target.
Every turn (including this), you can use 0.5 Actions to make a Spell Attack against that target.
Deals 1d10 Electric damage when it hits.
If you don't do the Spell Attack, the spell ends.

[Frost Nova] : 1 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge
All creatures within 2 meters of you make a Dexterity save.
They take 1d8 + 4 Cold damage if they fail, or half of that if they succeed.
If they fail, they are also Slowed.
_Variant: Fire Nova or Electric Nova._

[Frost Rune] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 1 meter
Duration: 8 hours
Place a rune on a smooth solid surface. The rune covers a 1x1 area.
Heavy physical contact with the rune detonates it, dealing 4d6 Cold damage (against Defense) to the creature that activates it.
The creature also rolls a Dexterity save.
If it fails, it is Snared (frozen).

[Explosion Rune] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 1 meter
Duration: 8 hours
Place a rune on a smooth solid surface. The rune covers a 1x1 area.
Heavy physical contact with the rune detonates it, dealing 5d6 Fire (or Force) damage (against Defense) to the creature that activates it.

[Fire Ball] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 3 Charges
Range: 6 meters (to the center)
Damage all creatuers in a 3x3x3 cube for 5d6 Fire damage (against Defense).
_Variants: Water Ball or Thunder Ball._

[Ice Spike] : 1 Action
Cost: 2 Charges.
Range: 6 meters
The target rolls a Dexterity save.
If it fails, it takes 3d6 + 2 Cold damage, or half otherwise.
The damage is doubled if the target is affected by any type of Crowd Control!

[Flameblade] : Passive
You can freely change the damage of your melee weapon attacks to Fire damage.

[Fire Enchantment] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Duration: 5 landed attacks, or 24 hours
Using a {Fire Rune}, touch a weapon to imbue it with Fire.
That weapon deals +2 Fire damage.

[Heat Metal] : 0.5 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 6 meters
Choose a piece of metal and heat it.
- If it's an armor, the wearer takes 2d6 + 1 Fire damage.
- If it's a weapon, the wearer takes 4 Damage and must succeed a Fortitude save, or becomes Crippled.

[Flame Strike] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters (to the center)
Duration: 1 minute or until cancelled
Conjure a 4x4 zone of flames on the ground.
At the end of each of your turns, roll 1d4 + 1.
All creatures inside the zone take that amount as Fire damage.

[Arcane Spear] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
All creatures in a 6 meter line from you make a Dexterity save.
Deal 10 damage to all targets who failed.
If no creature failed, you gain the Charge back.

[Phase Orb] : 0.5 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Pick a 1x1 zone. A magical orb is created in that place.
At the start of your next turn, the orb dashes to your position.
All creatures in the orb's path take 3d4 Force damage.

[Scorching Rays] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Make 3 attacks. Each does 1d6 + 5 Fire damage (vs Defense).
_Variant: Frostbiting Rays or Electric Rays._

[Water Sphere] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Shoot a 1 meter in diameter ball of water in a direction parallel to the floor that can travel up to 9 meters.
The ball can bounce off of walls and solid objects (not creatures).
The angle it bounces to is equal to the angle it was thrown from respective to the surface it bounces from (like light is reflected in a mirror in real life).
The ball bursts when it collides with a creature, dealing 2d10 damage plus 1d10 for each time it bounced.
(Damage is dealt against Defense).
The Sphere canb bounce up to 2 times.

	Arcane =<

[Dancing Blade] : Passive
You magically levitate your 1-Handed weapon to extend its range to 2 meters.
_It does not count as occupying your hand._

[Prestidigitation] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
You can create harmless sensory effects.
You can create sparks and change the temperature of objects (channeled).
You can instantly clean or soil objects.
You can make symbols appear on surfaces (up to 1 hour).
You can create small, harmless illusions that fit into your hand.
You can control a quill with telekinesis to quickly write with it (1 meter range).

[Thaumaturgy] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
You create one of the following magical effects:
- Your voice booms up to three times as loud as normal for 1 minute.
- You cause flames to flicker, brighten, dim, or change color for 1 minute.
- You cause harmless tremors in the ground for 1 minute.
- You cause an unlocked door or window to fly open or slam shut.
- You alter the appearance of your eyes for 1 minute.
At level 5 you can cast this for 0.5 Actions, and at Level 9, for 0 Actions.

[Feather Fall] : Reaction or 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: meters
Consume a 10 gold worth for an {Arcane Feather}.
Up to 2 creatures you choose will start falling gently, down to 3 meters per round.
Once the fall finishes, the spell effect ends.
_Arcane Feathers can be bought in most towns._
_For faster gameplay, you can spend 10 gold when casting the spell, and buy the feather later._

[Unlock] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 9 meters
You can attempt to unlock a lock magically.
Using this counts as if you Know How to Pick Locks.
You roll a Sleight of Hand for this, and you get a +2 on it.

[Sense Magic] : 0 Actions (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge.
You can tell if there is magic within (Intelligence * 10) meters from you (at least 10 meters).
You can also tell if an object you touch is magical.
If it is a common magic item, you can tell what it does (e.g. Potions, Scrolls, etc).

[Invisibility] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge.
You become invisible for up to (Intelligence) minutes (at least 1).
Attacking, taking damage or casting spells while invisible breaks the invisibility.

[Flash] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Instantly teleport up to 6 meters away.
You take with you all items carried.

[Levitate] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge.
First, roll 2d100. That's how many kilograms you can levitate when you cast this.
You can levitate one object you can see that remains within 6 meters of you.
You can slowly move the object vertically, 1 meter for each 2 of your movement points spent to levitate.
If you use this on an enemy, it must fail a Fortitude save to be levitated, and can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns.
A levitated creature counts as Heavy Snared.
Losing concentration (taking damage or using any action) instantly starts slowly dropping the target and breaks the Levitate.

[Water Walk] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 3 meters
Duration: 1 hour
Up to 2 willing creatures can walk on water as normally.
The effect ends if the creature takes damage.

[Spider Climb] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge
Duration: 10 minutes
Your hands and feet can stick to solid, (more or less) flat surfaces, so you can walk on them.
Your speed, if you do this, is halved.

	Protection =<

[Resistance] : 1 Action
Duration: Until their next rest
Touch an ally. It gains +1d4 on a saving throw of your choice.
You can only have this buff active on one person at a time.

[Absorb Element] : Reaction
Cost: 1 Charge.
Use when you take magic damage.
You only take half of that damage, and you infuse yourself with that element.
The next time you make a single target attack, that attack deals 1d6 extra damage of the infused element (if it hits).

[Alarm] : 10 minutes
Cost: 1 Charge.
Duration: 8 hours
You setup an invisible 4x4x4 meter cube.
If an unknown creature or enemy enters the cube, it alerts you mentally with a bell sound, or whatever sound you choose.

[Antimagic Rune] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge.
Place a rune on the ground.
You can activate it at any time and from any distance.
When you activate it, the rune creates a 5x5 meter zone.
ALL units inside are attempted a silence and take (Your Level) Arcane damage on detonation.

[Dispel Magic] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge
Touch an ally and dispell any ongoing curse or negative magical effects on it.

[Conceal Magic] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge.
Your magic becomes undetectable for 1 hour.

[Mage Armor] : Passive
While you are at 50% or more Health, your Defense can't be lower than 4.

[Barkskin] : Reaction
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 6 meters
That ally becomes Snared in the ground for up to 1 minute.
While rooted, that ally's Armor can't be lower than 6.
_On your turn, you can cancel this effect at any point._

[Shield Element] : 0.5 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Name 1 element from Fire/Cold/Electric and choose two allies.
The next time those allies would take that type of damage, that damage is halved.

[Wild Heart] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Up to 2 allies you touch gain +3 maximum (and current) Health and +1 to all saves until their next Long Rest.
_The bonus to Health and Saves doesn't stack with itself, and the bonus to Saves doesn't stack with other Save buffs._

	Conjuration =<
[Conjure Ammo] : 1 Action
Duration: 1 hour
You can conjure up to 10 arrows, bolts or bullets, javelins, throwing knives, etc.
_Tip: If you have this, you can just say you keep X amount of conjured ammo always ready, to speed the game up and not constantly interrupt the game._

[Light] : 1 Action
Range: 1 meter
Duration: 1 hour (8 at level 5, 24 at level 10)
You create a magical light bulb that sheds light in a 6 meters radius (30 meters at Level 5).
The bulb levitates and stays where you put it, and you can always move it around with your hands.
You can also simply attach the bulb to any object, or make it levitate and follow a willing creature.
Lasts until you cancel it or cast Light again, or is purposefully destroyed with an attack.
You choose the color of the light.
_The bulb can't go through solid objects._

[Chaining Grasp] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Extend a magical chain to a medium (or larger) living or undead creature and pull yourself to the target instantly.
The target also takes 1d6 True damage.

[Animate Dead] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Duration: 1 hour
Raise an undead creature to fight for you from a nearby medium or larger corpse. The corpse is then consumed.
The undead creature always has Medium size, 10 Maximum Health and 3 Defense.
It has saves equal to yours, but -2 to all skill checks.
It has 4 meters movement speed.
It can use 1 Action to attack and deals 1d8 damage if it hits.
Your undead takes its turn immediately before or after your turn (you choose).
The undead will always try to move to and attack the closest enemy to it (you choose if it's a tie).
You can use 0.5 Actions on your turn to command (one of) your undead to move to a certain location.

[Dancing Weapon] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 3 meters
Duration: 1 minute
You conjure a levitating, self-wielding melee weapon.
On your turn, the weapon can make a melee Spell Attack which deals 1d8 + 1 Force damage if it hits.
_The weapon has no special effect, it has 8 Defense and 1 Health._
_The weapon disappears if it moves more than 9 meters away from you._

[Distort Vision] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 3 meters
This incantation distorts the visual space in a 2x2x2 area.
Make a Deception check for this, and add your Intelligence to the roll.
While keeping the distorsion, you must maintain concentration.
You lose concentration if you take damage or become crowd controlled.

[Eldritch Steed] : 5 minutes (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge
Duration: 8 hours
Requires: Training in Riding
You summon a ridable animal which doubles your movement speed on land.
The animal takes either a spectral, demonic or skeletal form.
It follows the normal rules for Riding.
It does not participate in combat.
At Level 8 the Steed can fly.

[Fairy Fire] : 0.5 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Duration: 10 minutes
Declare a 4x4x4 meter zone.
All creatures in that zone make a Dexterity save with Disadvantage.
If they fail, they become outlined in a colored glow (you choose the color).
Those creatures can't benefit from normal Cover.
The creatures glow even through walls, but the denser the walls, the less they appear to glow.
A 1-meter thick concrete wall completely blocks the glow.

[Fake Sound] : 0.5 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 3 meters
Duration: 6 seconds max (30 at Level 4, 1 hour at Level 8)
You create a sound out of thin air.
Roll a Deception check and add your Intelligence modifier to it to see how accurate your sound is.

[Fog Cloud] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: all zone within 9 meters
A 5x5x5 meter cube of fog forms.
All creatures inside the fog benefit from Cover (-2), as well as creatures outside the fog attacked by one inside the fog.
You can choose the color of the fog.
The fog spreads around corners.

[Force Wall] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: within 6 meters
Duration: 1 minute
You create a 3x3 meter transparent, magical and thin force wall.
Creatures behind the wall benefit from Hard Cover (-5 to attacks against them through the wall).

[Conjure Object] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 3 meters
You can conjure an object that is up to 1x1x1 meter in size.
The object is solid and glows faintly.
You can't conjure mechanical objects.
The object disappears after 1 hour.

[Mage Hand] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Duration: 8 hours
Conjure a spectral hand in a place you can see.
You can move the hand as you move your normal hands and instead of spending movement points to move yourself, you can move your hand the same distance.
The hand can't attack and disappears if it goes outside a 12 meters range.

[Magic Prison] : 5 minutes (Level 8)
Cost: 2 Charges
You create a 4x4x4 meter cube.
Choose a creature inside the cube.
It makes an Intelligence save with triple disadvantage.
If it fails, it can not exit the cube for 24 hours.
It can make this save to try to escape every 24 hours.
You can cast this once per day per charge.

[Portal] : 4 Hours (Level 8)
Cost: 3 Charges.
Duration: 30 days
You need a partner who can cast Portal to cast this.
You both cast it at the same time, in different locations.
At the end of the cast time, a portal opens at the locations of the two casters.
Stepping through one of the portals (obviously) teleports the creature to the other portal.
You can only cast this every 30 days.

[Web] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 9 meters to center
Duration: 1 minute
Declare a 4x4 meter zone
The zone becomes filled with spider web and counts as Difficult Terrain.
If a creature exits the zone, it makes a Dexterity save.
If it fails, it is Snared immediately after it exits the zone.

[Shadow Clone] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 3 meters
Duration: 10 minutes
You create an illusion of yourself at a target location.
The illusion has 1 Health and 1 Armor and it fails all saves.
You can control what the illusion does at will.

[Watcher Eye] : 0 Actions (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 6 meters
You conjure a magical eye that records everything it sees for up to 24 hours.
After 24 hours, the eye stops recording.
You can use your magic to scan what the eye recorded in your mind.

	Mysticism =<
[Mind Blast] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 3 meters
A creature makes a Wisdom save.
If it fails, it takes 2d6 + 2 Psychic damage and is Feared.
After you cast Mind Blast on a creature, it becomes completely immune to Mind Blast for 1 day.

[Encode Thoughts] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Duration: 8 hours
You pull a String of Memory or Thoughts (can be visual, auditive, sensory and olphactive) from your own memory.
This String can be up to 1 minute in length.
It is visible to the eye as an arcane ribbon-like energy.
You can transport this String like you would carry a normal 10x10x10 decimeter object.
To view the String, a person can willingly absorb it through the mouth, eyes, nose, etc (corresponding to the type of memories) and visualize the String.
After that, the String disappears.
Encoding another String makes the previous String disappear.

[Charming Word] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Range: 6 meters
A creature makes a Wisdom save.
If it fails, all your Charisma checks for/against it have +2.
Lasts until the creature can't see or hear you anymore.

[Influence Beast] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 12 meters
A non-legendary beast that can see you makes a Charisma save.
If it fails, you can change the beast's mood (make it hungry, afraid, nervous, etc).

[Wild Steed] : 5 minutes (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge
Duration: Until you dismount
Requires: Training in Riding
You call upon a ridable land animal native to the zone you are in.
You and up to 1 more party member can ride this animal.
It follows the normal rules for Riding.
It does not participate in combat.
At Level 8, this can be a flying animal.

[Guidance] : Reaction
Range: 6 meters
When an ally makes an ability check, it can use your modifier instead of its own.
You can only do this every 1 hour.

[Mastery] : Passive
You can add your Wisdom or Charisma to an attack roll with a weapon instead of Strength or Dexterity.

[Translation] : 0 Actions (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge
Duration: 1 Hour
You can understand any spoken or written Common language.

[Speak With Animals] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Duration: 10 minutes (1 hour at level 4)
You can attempt to speak concrete ideas with willing, non-hostile animals.
Keep in mind that animals generally have low Intelligence, and they are not expected to 'speak' back to you through language.

[Speak With Plants] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Duration: 10 minutes (1 hour at level 6)
You can attempt to speak concrete ideas with willing, large plants.
Keep in mind that plants generally have low Intelligence, and they are not expected to 'speak' back to you through language.

	Restoration =<
[Cleansing Flames] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 9 meters
Make a spell attack.
If it hits, deals 2d4 Fire damage and removes all buffs and debuffs from the target.	
	
[Detect Poison and Disease] : 1 Action
You know if there is any poison (or poisonous creature) or disease (or ill person) within 6 meters.

[Purify Food and Drink] : 1 Action
Food you touch becomes clean of microbes and mild poison.
A water containter you touch (up to 2 liters per cast) becomes drinkable, clean and free of microbes and mild poison.

[Cure Wounds] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge
Touch an ally and heal it for 2d4 + Main Stat.

[Cure Poison] : 0 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge
Touch an ally and end any ongoing poison effects on it.

[Cure Disease] : 10 minutes
Cost: 1 Charge
Touch an ally and cure a normally curable disease from it.
At level 4, you can cure harder and stranger diseases.
At level 8, you can cure terminal or chronic diseases.
	
[Mending] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Touch a slightly broken piece of material that is still in one piece.
You can repair the damage done to it.
_Examples: repair a hole through a chain shirt or textile material, the crack in a piece of glass, etc._

[Spare the Dying] : 1 Action (Level 4)
Cost: 1 Charge.
Touch a creature that died at most 10 minutes ago or an unconscious creature.
If the creature is dead, and if the creature's body is fairly intact, the dead creature comes back to life, stabilized and unconscious.
If the creature is unconscious, the creature wakes up and is healed for 1 Health.

	Eldritch =<
	
[Hollow Touch] : 1 Action
Range: 3 meters
Make a Spell attack.
If you hit, deal 3d6 + 1 Necrotic damage and the target is unable to reagain health until the end of its next turn.

[Eldritch Blast] : 1 Action
Range: 6 meters
Make a Spell attack.
If you hit, deal 2d10 + 2 Force damage.

[Curse of Pain] : 0.5 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Duration: 1 minute
Declare a target.
Whenever it makes a weapon attack, it takes 1d8 Psychic damage.

[Curse of Weakness] : 0.5 Actions
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
Duration: 1 minute
A target makes a Charisma save.
If it fails, whenever it makes an attack roll or save, it rolls 1d4 and subtracts it from the result.

[Deathfire Bolt] : 1 Action
Cost: 1 Charge.
Range: 6 meters
